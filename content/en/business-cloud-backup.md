faq_items:
  - question: "Why do I need an external backup if I'm already using Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace?"
    answer: "Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace guarantee service availability (uptime), but not the recovery of your data in case of accidental deletion, ransomware attack, human error or account removal. Their shared responsibility model leaves data protection to the customer. An independent external backup is the only way to protect that information against incidents."
  - question: "What is the shared responsibility model in the cloud?"
    answer: "It is the model by which providers like Microsoft, Google or AWS take responsibility for keeping the infrastructure available and secure, while the customer is responsible for protecting, retaining and recovering their own data. This means that if you delete an email or file by mistake and it goes beyond the recycle bin period, the provider cannot recover it: you need your own backup."
  - question: "How long are backup copies retained?"
    answer: "We configure retention based on your business and compliance needs. By default we offer retentions of 7 to 15 days for anti-deletion protection (Insider Protection recycle bin) and configurable indefinite retentions for full copies. For regulatory compliance (ENS, NIS2, fiscal) we can keep copies for years."
  - question: "Does the backup protect against ransomware attacks?"
    answer: "Yes. Our copies are stored in immutable S3 repositories with logical air-gap, meaning that even if an attacker gains access to your systems with maximum privileges, they cannot modify or delete the copies during the configured immutability period. In addition, copies are deduplicated and encrypted with AES 256-bit."
  - question: "What's the difference between Veeam and beclone?"
    answer: "They are complementary technologies. Veeam is the enterprise standard for Microsoft 365 backup, virtual infrastructure (VMware/Hyper-V) and disaster recovery. Beclone is a specialised solution from Brontobyte Cloud for backing up mailboxes and files in Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace, with a simpler and more affordable model. We choose the technology based on your volume, recovery needs and budget."
  - question: "Do we comply with NIS2, ENS and ISO 27001 with your backup?"
    answer: "Yes. Brontobyte Cloud, our infrastructure partner, is certified in ENS (Spanish National Security Framework) and ISO 27001. Data is stored in data centres located in Spain, copies are immutable and traceable, and we have the documentation required for NIS2, ENS, GDPR and other compliance audits."
  - question: "How much does a professional enterprise backup cost?"
    answer: "The price depends on data volume, number of users or VMs, desired retention and service level. We work with monthly licences per VM (Veeam) or per user (beclone Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace), with no long-term commitments. Request information with no obligation and we'll send you a proposal tailored to your business."
  - question: "Can I recover a single email or file, or do I have to restore everything?"
    answer: "One of the key advantages of professional backup with Veeam and beclone is granular recovery: you can restore a single email, a specific file, a SharePoint folder, a calendar, contacts, etc. You don't need to restore the entire system to recover an individual item."

<ul class="mb-4 landing-text-muted">
<li><strong>Accidental or intentional deletions</strong> that exceed recycle bin retention periods.</li>
<li><strong>Ransomware attacks</strong> that encrypt both productive data and internal copies.</li>
