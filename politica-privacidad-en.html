Privacy Policy

At Qualiteasy Internet Solutions S.L., we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us, as ensuring and safeguarding the privacy and confidentiality of your personal data is fundamental to us. This is in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU Regulation 2016/679) (GDPR) and the Organic Law 3/2018, of December 5, on the Protection of Personal Data and Guarantee of Digital Rights (LOPDGDD).

This statement establishes our privacy and data protection policy regarding the information you provide us, ensuring that: i) at any time, you may request the deletion of your personal data, provided that the legal requirements for data protection and retention are met, ii) we are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of the collected personal data, ensuring their protection and not disclosing them to third parties without the explicit consent of the data owner, iii) we have implemented the necessary technical and organizational measures to ensure an adequate level of security for the detected risk.

Purpose of Data Processing

The collection and use of personal data are only possible if you voluntarily provide them to us. The different purposes of processing your personal data are:

  • Service Contracting (“Contracting”)

Personal data provided during the contracting of our services will be collected, processed, and used to manage the contracting process, execute the contract, and maintain the contractual relationship.

  • Satisfaction Surveys

Personal data provided during the contracting process will be used to improve our services and design our commercial offers through satisfaction surveys.

  • Commercial Contact

Personal data provided in web contact forms and quote request forms will be used to manage inquiries and provide requested quotations.

  • Commercial Communications

If you provide your explicit and unequivocal consent, the personal data collected will be processed and used to send you communications related to the products and services marketed by Qualiteasy.

Data Retention Period

Personal data for contracting purposes will be retained while the business relationship is maintained and for a maximum of 5 years.

Personal data for other purposes will be retained until the data owner exercises their right to deletion or revocation of consent.

Legal Basis for Processing

The legal basis for processing personal data for contracting is the fulfillment of legal obligations under the signed contract.

For satisfaction surveys, the legal basis is Qualiteasy’s legitimate interest in improving its products.

For commercial contact and communications, the legal basis is the explicit consent provided by the data owner.

Data Recipients

Qualiteasy will not disclose personal data to third parties except where required to comply with a legal obligation.

Exercising Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, and object to the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, contact Qualiteasy with the reference "GDPR Rights" via email at qualiteasy@qualiteasy.com.

Complaints to the Data Protection Authority
