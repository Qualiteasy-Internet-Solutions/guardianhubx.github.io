At GuardianHubX, our security strategy is based on three fundamental pillars: prevention, detection and recovery. We implement advanced solutions to protect the data and systems of our clients from any cyber threat.

🔒 Prevention

Preventing threats from reaching systems is the essential first step toward strong cybersecurity. We rely on advanced solutions such as:

  • Faronics – Protection against unwanted changes
  • NordLayer – Remote access security and network protection
  • Outkept – Practical phishing awareness training
  • NordPass – Secure password management
  • Passwork – Centralized credential management
  • Qualiteasy – Document management and regulatory compliance (ISO, GDPR, etc.)

🛡️ Detection

Detecting threats in real-time is key to mitigating risks before they cause damage. We use tools like:

  • Edgewatch – Continuous threat monitoring
  • Passwork – Access control and password usage tracking
  • NordPass – Security alerts and audit capabilities
  • Faronics Antivirus – Active malware protection

🔄 Recovery

In the event of a security incident, recovery capabilities are crucial for minimizing operational impact. We offer solutions such as:

  • Faronics Cloud – Rapid system restoration
  • iDrive – Automatic backup with efficient recovery
  • Prey Project – Device protection and location tracking
