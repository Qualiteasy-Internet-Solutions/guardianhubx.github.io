Faronics

Endpoint protection and device management for businesses

30,000+ customers · 10 million licences · 150 countries · Rated 4.7/5

Deep Freeze

Freezes the system state. Any change — including ransomware, malware and zero-day threats — disappears on reboot. Reduces IT support tickets by up to 60%.

Software Updater / Deploy

Centralised patch and update management for Windows and third-party applications. No site visits, no manual intervention on each device.

Anti-Executable

Blocks any non-approved application from running. If it is not on the whitelist, it does not execute — full stop. One of the most effective defences against ransomware.

MDM

Cloud-based management of Apple, Android and Chromebook devices from a single dashboard. Full control over corporate and BYOD devices.

WINSelect + Remote Connect

Restrict access to applications, websites and system settings. Browser-based remote access to any managed device without additional software.

Power Save + Usage Stats

Intelligent energy consumption management and detailed software and device usage analytics. Cut costs and make decisions based on real data.

Who is Faronics for?

  • Multi-user environments — schools, universities, libraries, training centres and foundations where computers are shared by multiple users and must always be clean and operational
  • Businesses with many endpoints — centralised patch and update management without visiting each workstation
  • High-turnover environments — hotels, clinics, public service points where every user leaves a trace
  • Small IT teams — manage dozens or hundreds of devices from a single panel, reducing support workload by up to 60%
  • Ransomware-risk environments — Deep Freeze eliminates any threat on the next reboot, with no manual disinfection needed
