2. Subject matter

This Agreement covers the provision of the Guardian Radar service, consisting of an external digital risk monitoring system designed to detect potential information exposures, compromised credentials or domain impersonation associated with THE CLIENT.

3. General service description

Guardian Radar is a preventive digital surveillance service that analyses information from public breach databases, compromised credential repositories on the Dark Web, domain registries and open Internet sources.

Preventive alerts: The system detects matches between these sources and the identifiers provided by THE CLIENT. When a potential risk is detected, the system generates an informational alert.

Support and "Panic Button": The service includes a direct communication channel (via WhatsApp or similar) for quick queries about suspicious emails or links (phishing). This support consists of a quick visual and preventive analysis and does not replace a forensic analysis, code audit or technical response to complex incidents.
